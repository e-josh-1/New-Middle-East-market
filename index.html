Our Story

A souk, a butcher shop, and a kitchen — built by one family

Since 1992, generations of Ottawa families have crossed our threshold for the same reason their parents did: a butcher counter that treats every cut like it's for their own table, a grill that never stops smoking, and shelves stacked floor to ceiling with the spices, cheeses, and breads of home.

We're not a chain, and we never wanted to be. We're the shop where the person behind the counter remembers your order before you finish asking — and where "we're out of that" is answered with "let me check in the back."

"Ask for Ahmed at the grill. Ask for Mustafa at the counter. Everyone who shops here has a regular."
